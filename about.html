About Us

Park City Express is owner operated and we take pride in treating our passengers like family. We primarily operate throughout the Park City area with transportation to and from Salt Lake International. You can always count on Park City Express to get you where you need to be on time, with our 98% on-time rating.

Our Services Include:

  • Private shuttle services between Salt Lake International and the Park City Area
  • Private shuttle service to ski resorts
  • Around Town Transportation Services
  • Group transportation services (Call to Book)
  • Private Ride Services
  • Executive Ride Services

Our Vehicles:

  • Shuttle Vans
  • Private Vans
  • Private SUVs
  • Large Passenger Vans
  • Private Executive Cars

Our transportation experts are available 24/7 for your convenience. When you're flying into Salt Lake International or if you're returning from Park City, don't leave your transportation up to chance. Park City Express will get you there promptly, safely and comfortably.

Our Mission

Park City Express' mission is to provide excellent shuttle service through our on-time pick up and drop off, with a promise that you will receive a private ride at a shared price. Our satisfaction comes from putting the customer first.

Our Drivers

At Park City Express, our commitment to the highest safety standards is evident in our driver screening and training procedures. Prior to hiring a potential driver, we verify the current validity of his or her Driver’s License. We ensure that the driver has undergone state-regulated training and evaluation, including expert knowledge of the vehicles he or she drives and a thorough understanding of safety procedures and defensive driving habits. The driver must have no negative points on his or her license, and must possess a valid Airport Driver Access Badge, ensuring clear background checks through the Department of Homeland Security.

Park City Express maintains a strict zero tolerance policy on drug and alcohol abuse. All applicants for employment must undergo DOT-regulated drug and alcohol screening prior to any offer of employment. Once employed, all drivers are subject to ongoing random DOT-regulated drug and alcohol screening as long as they are employed with Park City Express.

This rigorous approach has proven successful by our low turnover of employees, some of whom have been with us since the inception of our company. Park City Express has the utmost confidence that our professional drivers are the best in the Northern Utah Metropolitan Areas. World leaders, VIPs, international corporations, churches, schools, and families have trusted our drivers to get them where they need to go, and not a single passenger has ever been let down.

If you are interested in joining our team, please visit our careers page.

SMOKING: Smoking is not permitted in any of our vehicles.

TRAVELING WITH CHILDREN: Park City Express welcomes the opportunity to serve our younger travelers when accompanied by a responsible adult. It is the parents’ or legal guardian’s responsibility to become knowledgeable about the applicable laws in the states in which they will be traveling. Operators reserve the right to refuse service to parties out of compliance with state laws.
