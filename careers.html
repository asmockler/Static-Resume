Careers

Welcome and thank you for your interest in working with Park City Express!

At Park City Express, we strive to help you be in business for yourself, but not BY yourself. We are always looking for new talent that shares our enthusiasm in working hard while upholding the highest standard of safety and professionalism in handling our vehicles and, especially, our customers.

With that said, we are excited to have you join our team of drivers! The following instructions contain all the information you need to begin working with us:

  1. Obtain a Salt Lake City Corporation Ground Transportation Driver Badge (Process Listed Below)
  2. Agree to wear the company uniform.
  3. Agree to abide by all the provisions of the company contract, information sheets, memos and operation manual.
  4. Maintain the company standard of appearance.
  5. Maintain a vehicle that is free of smoke (this includes the smell of smoke – an SLC Corp Rule)
  6. Work a 10-12 hour shift and your regular schedule must include a Saturday or Sunday.

How to obtain a Salt Lake City Corporation Ground Transportation Driver Badge

To begin working as a driver, you must obtain a Salt Lake City Corporation Ground Transportation Driver Badge. The airport badge certifies ground transportation drivers to pick up and drop off passengers in and out of Salt Lake International. If you do not have a badge, please take the time to carefully review and fill out the application below while following each step as follows.

  1. To begin the process, please call the Salt Lake Ground Transportation badging office at (801) 575-2423 or go to their online scheduling tool to schedule a fingerprinting appointment. You will need 2 valid forms of I.D. for this appointment. Once your appointment is scheduled with the badging office, please contact us and let us know when your appointment is scheduled.
  2. After you have scheduled your fingerprinting appointment, fill out the Ground Transportation Driver Badge Application. Please, carefully fill out sections 1, 3, 4, and 5.
  3. To finish, please make sure you have carefully read through and complete the final two steps listed on the Ground Transportation Drivers information page.

And there you have it! We hope our step-by-step process has been simple and effective. If you have any questions or need any assistance with the process, please don’t hesitate to ask. We have gone through the same procedure and are willing to assist in any way that we can.

We look forward to having you with us at Park City Express!
