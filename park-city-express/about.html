Our Drivers

At Shuttle Express, our commitment to the highest safety standards is evident in our driver screening and training procedures. Prior to hiring a potential driver, we verify the current validity of his or her Driverâ€™s License. We ensure that the driver has undergone state-regulated training and evaluation, including expert knowledge of the vehicles he or she drives and a thorough understanding of safety procedures and defensive driving habits. The driver must have no negative points on his or her license, and must possess a valid Airport Driver Access Badge, ensuring clear background checks through the Department of Homeland Security.

Shuttle Express maintains a strict zero tolerance policy on drug and alcohol abuse. All applicants for employment must undergo DOT-regulated drug and alcohol screening prior to any offer of employment. Once employed, all drivers are subject to ongoing random DOT-regulated drug and alcohol screening as long as they are employed with Shuttle Express.

This rigorous approach has proven successful by our low turnover of employees, some of whom have been with us since the inception of our company. Shuttle Express has the utmost confidence that our professional drivers are the best in the Northern Utah Metropolitan Areas. World leaders, VIPs, international corporations, churches, schools, and families have trusted our drivers to get them where they need to go, and not a single passenger has ever been let down.
