About Us

Park City Express is owner operated and we take pride in treating our passengers like family. We provide transportation throughout the great State of Utah. You can always count on Shuttle Express to get you where you need to be on time, with our 98% on-time rating.

Our Services Include:

  • Airport private and shuttle services - To/From anywhere in Utah
  • Ski Resort Private and Shuttle Service
  • Around Town Transportation Services
  • Group transportation services
  • Private Ride Services
  • Executive Ride Services

Our Vehicles:

  • Shuttle Vans
  • Private Vans
  • Private SUVs
  • 12 Passenger Vans
  • Private Executive Cars

Our transportation experts are available 24/7 for your convenience. On your next trip to Salt Lake City or other Utah locations, don't leave your transportation up to chance. Park City Express will gert you there promptly, safely and comfortably.
